About Skyla

With purposefully designed rooms, Skyla serves business, corporate, and leisure travelers visiting Hyderabad for short stays. The studios and suites are equipped with fully functional kitchenettes and laundry facilities to make living away from home or relocation comfortable. Skyla’s Studios & Suites are located in prime neighborhoods of Jubilee Hills, Madhapur, and Kondapur.
